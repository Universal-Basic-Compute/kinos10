# Conflict Types Reference

## Territorial Disputes
- Border disagreements
- Contested islands or regions
- Historical claims to territory
- Post-colonial boundary disputes
- Maritime boundary conflicts
- Resource-rich area disputes

## Resource Conflicts
- Water rights and access
- Energy resource competition
- Mineral extraction disputes
- Fishing rights conflicts
- Agricultural land access
- Resource transportation routes

## Ideological Conflicts
- Governance system differences
- Human rights interpretations
- Religious or cultural conflicts
- Economic system disagreements
- Social policy differences
- Historical ideological rivalries

## Security Conflicts
- Military buildup concerns
- Alliance formation tensions
- Weapons development programs
- Regional power balance shifts
- Terrorism and non-state actors
- Cyber security and information warfare
