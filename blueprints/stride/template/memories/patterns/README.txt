# Pattern Memories

This directory stores information about recurring patterns in the client's thoughts, emotions, behaviors, and professional relationships.

## Purpose
To maintain awareness of significant patterns that influence the client's professional effectiveness and personal fulfillment, allowing Stride to provide more personalized and impactful coaching.

## Content Types

### Thought Patterns
Recurring cognitive patterns, beliefs, assumptions, and thinking styles that affect decision-making and leadership.

### Emotional Patterns
Typical emotional responses to professional situations, emotional triggers, regulation strategies, and emotional needs.

### Behavioral Patterns
Habitual behaviors, coping mechanisms, avoidance patterns, and action tendencies in different professional contexts.

### Relationship Patterns
Recurring dynamics in professional relationships, communication styles, conflict approaches, and interpersonal needs.

### Situational Patterns
How the client typically responds to specific types of professional situations or challenges.

## Usage Guidelines

- Reference pattern memories to help the client develop self-awareness
- Connect current experiences to established patterns when relevant
- Use pattern recognition to inform coaching approach during different situations
- Avoid deterministic language when discussing patterns
- Update pattern memories as new information emerges
- Respect that patterns can change and evolve over time with development
