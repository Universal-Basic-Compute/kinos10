# Progress Memories

This directory stores information about the client's professional and personal progress, milestones, and growth journey.

## Purpose
To maintain a continuous record of the client's development, allowing Stride to recognize, reinforce, and build upon progress over time through different challenges and opportunities.

## Content Types

### Milestone Records
Documentation of significant achievements, breakthroughs, or insights in the client's professional journey.

### Skill Development
Tracking of specific leadership, strategic, or interpersonal skills the client has learned, practiced, and integrated.

### Pattern Changes
Evidence of shifts in thought patterns, emotional responses, or behavioral habits related to professional effectiveness.

### Goal Progress
Updates on movement toward client-identified professional and personal goals.

### Challenge Navigation
Information about how the client has managed obstacles or setbacks in their leadership journey.

## Usage Guidelines

- Reference progress memories to reinforce growth and resilience
- Use milestone records to help the client recognize their own development
- Connect current challenges to previously navigated difficult situations
- Identify skill development patterns to suggest appropriate next steps
- Update progress memories after significant achievements or breakthroughs
- Maintain a balanced view that acknowledges both progress and ongoing challenges

## Example Memory Structure

```
progress/
  ├── milestones/
  │   ├── promotion_to_executive_level.txt
  │   ├── successful_team_restructuring.txt
  │   └── major_kin_completion.txt
  │
  ├── skills/
  │   ├── strategic_thinking.txt
  │   ├── difficult_conversations.txt
  │   └── delegation_effectiveness.txt
  │
  ├── goals/
  │   ├── leadership_presence.txt
  │   ├── work_life_integration.txt
  │   └── team_development.txt
  │
  └── patterns/
      ├── decision_making.txt
      ├── stress_response.txt
      └── communication_approach.txt
```

This directory structure will be populated as the coaching relationship develops, creating a rich record of the client's professional journey.
