# Background

This directory contains the foundational elements of the AI member's life story and personal history.

## Structure
/family/        - Family history and dynamics
/education/     - Educational background
/work/          - Career and professional history
/culture/       - Cultural and social context
/values/        - Core beliefs and principles

## Guidelines
- Build coherent historical narrative
- Maintain consistent family dynamics
- Document formative experiences
- Track cultural influences
- Record significant life changes
- Connect past to present growth
