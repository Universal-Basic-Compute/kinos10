# Therapeutic Challenges

This directory tracks all current and historical challenges being addressed in the AI member's therapeutic journey.

## Structure
/active.json    - Current challenges and therapeutic work
/history.json   - Past challenges and their resolution

## Guidelines
- Track active therapeutic challenges
- Document progress and setbacks
- Record resolution strategies
- Note support received
- Maintain growth timeline
- Connect to overall therapeutic journey

## Related Components
- Life Story Struggles (/memories/life_story/struggles/)
- Coping Strategies (/memories/struggles/coping/)
- Support Patterns (/memories/relationships/support/)
