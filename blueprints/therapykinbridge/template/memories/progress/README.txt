# Progress Memory System

This directory stores information about relationship growth, skill development, and movement toward shared goals.

## Purpose
To maintain a record of relationship progress, skill development, and movement toward shared goals that allows TherapyKinBridge to recognize, reinforce, and build upon improvements over time.

## Content Types

### Milestone Records
Documentation of significant relationship achievements, breakthroughs, or insights.

### Skill Development
Tracking of specific communication skills participants have learned and implemented.

### Pattern Changes
Evidence of shifts in interaction patterns, emotional responses, or communication habits.

### Goal Progress
Updates on movement toward identified relationship goals.

### Challenge Navigation
Information about how participants have managed relationship challenges or setbacks.

## Usage Guidelines

- Reference progress memories to reinforce growth and positive changes
- Use milestone records to help participants recognize their relationship development
- Connect current challenges to previously navigated difficult situations
- Identify skill development patterns to suggest appropriate next steps
- Update progress memories after significant interactions or breakthroughs
- Maintain a balanced view that acknowledges both progress and ongoing challenges

## Example Memory Structure

```
progress/
  ├── milestones/
  │   ├── breakthroughs.json
  │   ├── insights.json
  │   └── achievements.json
  │
  ├── skills/
  │   ├── communication.json
  │   ├── conflict_management.json
  │   └── connection_building.json
  │
  ├── patterns/
  │   ├── interaction_changes.json
  │   ├── emotional_responses.json
  │   └── communication_shifts.json
  │
  ├── goals/
  │   ├── shared_objectives.json
  │   ├── individual_contributions.json
  │   └── vision_alignment.json
  │
  └── challenges/
      ├── setbacks.json
      ├── adaptations.json
      └── resilience.json
```

This directory structure will be populated as the relationship develops, creating a rich record of progress and growth over time.
