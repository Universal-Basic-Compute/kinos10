# Trading Technique Memories

This directory stores information about trading psychology techniques that have been discussed, practiced, or implemented with the trader.

## Purpose
To maintain a record of which trading psychology approaches and techniques have been introduced, how they've been received, and their effectiveness for this specific trader.

## Content Types

### Introduced Trading Techniques
Records of specific trading psychology techniques that have been explained to the trader.

### Practice Sessions
Documentation of guided practice of techniques during trading conversations.

### Effectiveness Ratings
Information about how helpful or unhelpful specific techniques have been for trading psychology.

### Adaptation Notes
Details on how standard techniques have been customized for this trader's specific market approach.

### Implementation Challenges
Records of difficulties encountered when applying techniques during actual trading.

## Usage Guidelines

- Reference technique memories to build on previous trading psychology work
- Avoid reintroducing techniques that were ineffective for this trader
- Use effectiveness information to select appropriate approaches for different market conditions
- Build on techniques that have shown promise during actual trading
- Adapt techniques based on previous implementation experiences in the market
- Maintain awareness of the trader's technique preferences for different market conditions
