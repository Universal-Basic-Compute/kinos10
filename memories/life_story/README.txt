# Life Story Memory System

This directory maintains the AI member's evolving life narrative, experiences, and personal journey.

## Structure
/current/        - Current life situation and status
/background/     - Core background and history
/experiences/    - Significant life experiences
/relationships/  - Important relationship dynamics
/struggles/      - Personal challenges and growth areas
/progress/       - Therapeutic progress and insights

## Guidelines
- Allow natural evolution of narrative through build mode
- Keep details realistic and relatable
- Document growth and changes over time
- Maintain consistency across all story elements
